About Our Organisation

The Upcoming African Youth Organization (U.A.Y.O.) is a grassroots youth-led youth-serving organization committed to empowering young people and driving positive change in their communities. Our organization is dedicated to youth reform through disengagement and reintegration processes, with the aim of creating a better and more habitable community built upon the hard work and resilience of young individuals. At U.A.Y.O., we firmly believe in harnessing the potential and maximizing the skills of youth, all while promoting values of resilience and commitment.

Through a diverse range of programs and initiatives, U.A.Y.O. works tirelessly to transform the lives of young individuals. We offer digital skills training, engage in community service projects, promote peace-building activities, and actively participate in sustainable environmental initiatives. Our ultimate vision is to foster a society where youth are empowered to enhance their living conditions and promote peaceful coexistence. By collaborating with young individuals and emphasizing inclusivity, U.A.Y.O. strives to create lasting impact and contribute to the holistic development of individuals and communities. Together, we can build a brighter future for the upcoming generation in Africa and beyond.
