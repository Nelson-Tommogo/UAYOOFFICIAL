Community Development

Our community development initiatives focus on fostering social cohesion and economic growth. We aim to empower local residents through skill-building workshops, access to resources, and collaborative projects that enhance the overall quality of life. By encouraging active participation, we ensure that every voice is heard in the decision-making process.

Youth Employability

We believe in the potential of our youth to drive change and contribute meaningfully to society. Our programs are designed to enhance their skills and improve their employability. Through mentorship, internships, and vocational training, we prepare young individuals for the job market, equipping them with the tools they need to succeed and build a sustainable future.

Giving Back to the Community

Giving back is at the heart of our mission. We engage in various outreach programs that provide essential services to underprivileged groups. Our initiatives include food drives, health screenings, and educational workshops aimed at uplifting the community. By fostering a culture of generosity, we aim to create a supportive environment where everyone can thrive.

Good Governance

Good governance is essential for sustainable development. We advocate for transparency, accountability, and community involvement in local governance. Through civic education and capacity-building initiatives, we empower citizens to engage actively in governance processes. Our aim is to foster a political environment where citizens have a voice and their rights are respected.

"Welcome to the Upcoming African Youth Organization (U.A.Y.O.)! We are a grassroots, youth-led organization dedicated to transforming the lives of young individuals and building a better future. At U.A.Y.O., we believe in the power of disengagement and reintegration to create a crime-free society. Our programs focus on increasing youth employability, promoting a culture of tolerance and perseverance, and utilizing art and culture to disseminate information. We are committed to providing platforms that empower young people to maximize their skills and potential, enabling them to create positive change in their communities. Join us on this journey towards a brighter tomorrow!"

    Our Activities

    This is what we do

    • Comunity Outreach Programs

      The program focuses on mentoring and supporting young people to understand themselves and build their capacity to deal with issues that affect them at a personal level and how to overcome them. It also focuses on matters that relate to skills, talents, and passion as a way of engaging young people and enabling them to earn a living. The program do conduct projects such as health-related, educational-related, character development-related, entrepreneurship training, skills training, and talent workshops. The program targets schools, community members, and correctional centers as the primary target audience.Read More

    • Agri-Jenga

      The program focuses on the agricultural sector as a way of creating opportunity to young people as a form of disengaging themselves and a soft power for reintegration process. The program focuses on alternative way of earning a living without relying on white-collar jobs. It is based on projects such as animal rearing, fruit planting, and crop farming. It also checks on modern ways of practicing agriculture and improving agricultural practices with the lead of youths. The program targets institutions and communities in both urban and rural areasRead More

    • Making the Environment Reliable (MER)

      The program focuses on the environmental sector as a way of making the community better and more habitable for living creatures through advocating for climate action in ending global warming and embracing projects such as proper waste management and disposal, tree planting, and recycling. It emphasizes on taking responsibility in matters related to reducing the rate of pollution, such as air pollution, water pollution, and land pollution, through working together in transforming. The program has been working with community members, institutions, and individual.Read More

    • Community Peace Iniatives

      U.A.Y.O. actively engages in promoting peace and resolving conflicts within the community. This can involve organizing peace dialogues, mediation sessions, and workshops on conflict resolution and intergroup understanding. U.A.Y.O. works closely with community leaders, stakeholders, and local organizations to create platforms for dialogue, foster empathy, and build bridges between different groups. By actively participating in community peace initiatives, U.A.Y.O. strives to cultivate an environment of peace, understanding, and unity.Read More

    • Sustainable Environmental Initiatives

      U.A.Y.O. can actively engage in activities aimed at promoting environmental sustainability and conservation. This can include organizing tree planting campaigns, beach clean-ups, recycling drives, and educational programs on climate change and environmental stewardship. By encouraging young individuals to take an active role in protecting and preserving the environment, U.A.Y.O. contributes to building a greener and more sustainable futureRead More

    • Digital Skills Training for Vulnerable Youths:

      U.A.Y.O. focuses on providing digital skills training to vulnerable youths in the society. This can include offering courses or workshops on computer literacy, coding, digital marketing, graphic design, and other relevant skills that are in demand in today's digital age. By equipping vulnerable youths with digital skills, U.A.Y.O. empowers them to access better educational and employment opportunities, bridge the digital divide, and enhance their overall digital literacy.Read More
